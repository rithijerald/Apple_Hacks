Guided Breathing Exercise

Find a comfortable position, close your eyes, and focus on your breathing. Follow these steps:

  1. Inhale deeply through your nose for 4 seconds.
  2. Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
  3. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds.
  4. Pause for 2 seconds before the next breath.

Stress Relief Exercise

Try this quick exercise to relieve stress:

  1. Find a quiet place to sit comfortably.
  2. Place your hands on your lap and close your eyes.
  3. Take 10 slow, deep breaths, focusing on the sensation of your breath.
  4. Visualize a peaceful place and imagine yourself there for a few minutes.
